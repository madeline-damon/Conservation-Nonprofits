Every day, NPR connects with millions of Americans on the air, online, and in person to explore the news, ideas, and what it means to be human. Through its network of member stations, NPR makes local stories national, national stories local, and global stories personal.      60 million  weekly audience across platforms 1  Listeners and readers experience NPR across a full range of platforms including radio, smart speakers, npr.org, social media, live events, NPR apps and podcasts.    1,000+  stations broadcasting NPR 2  Our network of local member stations collaborates with NPR to deliver an increasingly rare and vital mix of rigorously reported local and national stories.      35  bureaus worldwide  17 international and 17 domestic bureaus. Our teams in the U.S. and across the globe discover the most important stories wherever they are happening.    23 million  weekly on-air listeners 3  98.5% of the U.S. population lives within the listening area of a station carrying NPR programming; NPR's Morning Edition and All Things Considered are consistently among the most-listened-to news radio shows in America.      18.3 million  weekly website visitors 4  The latest news, award-winning digital journalism, multimedia visual experiences, live concerts, first listens and decades of program audio, all at npr.org.    2.6 million  monthly app users 5  The news you need and the stories you want, at your fingertips or in your pocket, wherever your day takes you.
